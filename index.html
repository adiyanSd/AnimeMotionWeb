Welcome to BUDDY ZONE

Buddy Zone is run by Mohammad Adyan and offers focused practice resources for NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling) students across India. Our Telegram bot @genie01_bot provides an interactive learning experience that includes:

  • Practice of previous-year board papers (NIOS) with step-by-step explanations.
  • Exam simulator mode to mimic real exam conditions and reduce time-pressure errors.
  • Regular quiz competitions to boost learning, engagement, and confidence.
  • Progress tracking and tips to help students improve scores efficiently.

Everything is delivered via our Telegram bot so students can practice anytime, anywhere. Payments for premium features are handled securely via Cashfree payment links.
