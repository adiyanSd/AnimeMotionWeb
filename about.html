About BUDDY ZONE

Helping NIOS students improve scores with smart practice

Who we are

Buddy Zone was founded by Mohammad Adyan to support NIOS students across India. We specialise in previous-year board paper practice, exam simulation, and competitive quizzes designed to build accuracy, speed, and confidence.

What we offer

  • Past Papers Practice: Carefully curated past-year question sets with step-by-step solutions.
  • Exam Simulator: Time-bound mock exams that recreate board exam conditions so students learn time management and reduce mistakes.
  • Quiz Competitions: Regular timed quizzes that encourage revision and increase engagement.
  • Progress Tracking: Simple reports and tips so students and parents can see improvement areas.

How it works

Students interact with our Telegram bot @genie01_bot to choose subjects, attempt tests, and join quizzes. For premium content and automated features, we provide secure payment links (Cashfree) that can be completed on mobile or web.

Our mission

To make board exam preparation simple, practice-focused, and accessible to all NIOS students so they can score better with less stress.
